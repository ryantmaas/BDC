Hook Up & Lead Wire


Rubber and plastic insulated single conductor hook-up and lead wire products are designed for reliable and consistent performance. For your quality assurance needs, these components are built to comply with rigid industry and government specifications.

In lead wire manufacturing, Belden controls the entire process turning process copper rod into tinned copper wire strands and mixing plastic and formulating rubber for conductor insulations. You can trust that you are getting consistently top quality wire.
